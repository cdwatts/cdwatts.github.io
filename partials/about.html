an introduction

Hi there! My name is Claire Darling Watts. I am the creator of this website and the human behind everything you see here. My work involves creative writing, frontend web development, user experience design, data visualization, travel photography and grassroots community organizing. As you can gather, I'm a bit of a human of all trades. I am somehow a logical thinker, an intuitive dreamer, a clear communicator and a detail-driven creator all at the same time. I think that's why I am able to thrive in creative environments that value innovation and ideation. Before I was called to pursue writing as my primary area of expertise, I began as a computer programmer and web designer. In 2018, I graduated with honors from the University of California, Santa Cruz with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Computer Science and Sustainability. While I do love the complex thinking and attention to detail that coding requires, I also consider myself to be especially talented in the art of writing. It is my most honest and powerful method of sharing ideas with the world. And I'd love the opportunity to help you express yours.

interests
